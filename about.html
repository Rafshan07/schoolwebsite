Sejarah dari SMP Negeri 1 Cibadak

History of SMP Negeri 1 Cibadak, Sukabumi Regency

SMP Negeri 1 Cibadak was established in 1965 based on the Decree of the Minister of Education and Culture of the Republic of Indonesia. Initially, the school was built in the Kebon Pala area, which is now the Cibadak Market & Terminal. Driven by the enthusiasm and concern of the community for education, this school was officially established with the name SMP Cibadak.
